Guidelines for Assessment

  • Read each question carefully.
  • Answer truthfully and to the best of your ability.
  • Make sure you are in a quiet and comfortable space.
  • Do not rush through the questions.
  • Take your time to think before submitting answers.

Consent for Data Collection & Usage

By proceeding with this assessment, you acknowledge and agree to the following:

  1. Data Collection & Storage: Your responses, including assessment scores, may be collected and stored along with optional identifying details (such as name and email) for evaluation purposes.
  2. Primary Purpose – Hiring Evaluations: The collected data is primarily used to assess job suitability for various roles.
  3. Secondary Purpose – Research & System Improvement: Data may be used for research to improve the assessment framework. Only anonymized data will be used.
  4. Data Protection & Confidentiality: Your data will be securely stored and will not be shared with third parties without your explicit consent.
  5. User Rights: You may request access to or deletion of your data at any time, subject to retention policies.
