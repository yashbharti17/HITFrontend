Guidelines for the Evaluation

  • Read each statement carefully before selecting a response.
  • Ensure that every item is answered before submitting.
  • There are no right or wrong answers—choose the response that best reflects your thoughts, feelings, or experiences.
  • Take your time—there is no time limit for this evaluation.
  • For the best insights, respond honestly rather than choosing what you think is the ‘ideal’ answer.

Consent to Data Collection & Use

By proceeding with this assessment, you acknowledge and agree to the collection, storage, and use of your data as outlined below:

  1. Data Storage & Access:
    • Personal details (Name, Education, etc.) will be stored alongside assessment results.
    • Only authorized personnel from our team will have access to this data for recruitment and evaluation purposes.
  2. Use for Hiring & Recruitment:
    • Assessment results and relevant professional details will be shared with hiring personnel from other companies to assist in candidate evaluation.
    • Hiring organizations will receive candidate reports but will not have direct access to stored data.
  3. Use for Research & Test Development:
    • Anonymized assessment data may be analyzed to improve the test’s accuracy and effectiveness.
    • No personally identifiable information will be included in research or shared externally.
  4. Data Protection & Confidentiality:
    • All collected data will be securely stored and used only for the stated purposes.
    • Candidates may request data deletion, subject to ongoing recruitment processes.
